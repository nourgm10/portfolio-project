Weather app

This website is my very first solo project from start to finish, including the design and code. I wanted to challenge myself right out of the gate by incorporating interesting, minimalist and modern design trends like neumorphism. Along the way, I picked up skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and even learned how to integrate APIs. Now, with this desktop app, anyone can easily check the 5-day weather forecast for any city in the world.

This second project slightly resembles the first, but I delved deeper into experimenting with diverse design trends. Through working on this project, I refined my proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, reinforcing and enhancing my understanding of these languages.
